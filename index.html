FEU Mission

FEU Roosevelt envisions a productive and responsible citizenry
empowered through education.

FEU Vision

  • Promotes the value of resilience through a dynamic curriculum and programs that advocate teamwork and collaboration.

  • Develops competence in lifelong learners; nurtures their ability to be creative, critical thinkers, and effective communicators.

  • Instills integrity of mind, body, and spirit throughout its community.
